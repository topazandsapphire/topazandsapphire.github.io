]]> Today we’re rounding up some of our favorite home office spaces in hopes to inspire you to spice up your at-work or at-home office space.

If you work from home, it is so important that you have an area carved out that sparks creativity, productivity, and efficiency. While I love to lounge and get things done from the comfort of my couch, I can oftentimes get distracted by household chores, Netflix, or other people that are in my working area. This is why its vital to have a space, even if it’s just a little nook, dedicated to getting your work done and not being distracted when you take conference calls remotely.
