About

Topaz & Sapphire is the creative inception of sisters Cassie and Latisha.

From an early age both had professional aspirations. As elementary school kids Cassie wanted to be a meteorologist, while Latisha wanted to be a pediatrician.

Times changed, they grew, and new ambitions were realized. Through various hairstyles and career possibilities, what stayed the same was their vision of a working woman with an inspiring home life. They believe life is about balance and that you can have a great career and pursue excellence in other areas outside of work simultaneously. T&S is a space that celebrates career, as well as recreational and home life.

Forget “having it all” and focus more on “giving it your all.” You’re a gem — life is what refines you.

Cassie, (aka Topaz), graduated from the Marriott School of Management at Brigham Young University with a degree in business marketing. In her down time Cassie spoils her pup Charlotte Barkley with new chew toys that are destroyed within 30 minutes and Snapchats 24/7. (Is Snapchat still a thing?) She is frequently packing her suitcase for her next adventure and though she’s married to a New Englander, she will forever root for her Carolina Panthers. She currently works as a media planner at a national media agency.
