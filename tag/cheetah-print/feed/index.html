]]> Though a self-proclaimed summer girl, I have to say there’s something so magical about the fall. The glow of the trees as the leaves change from green to yellow and orange to red, the crisp mornings, and the fog that hovers over the Puget Sound horizon are all some of my favorite things about autumn in Washington. No matter where I am living though, my love for fall fashion remains the same! While I am by no means a fashionista, I do enjoy shopping (what woman doesn’t love a little retail therapy) and expressing myself through the clothes that I wear. I love that fall fashion allows you to mix textures, prints, and layers without having to worry about bundling up for freezing temperatures, or losing layers for hot muggy summers.

This season I’m falling for cheetah print. I’ve always been a leopard print fan, but I’m loving all of the new ways I’m seeing it styled and used in different wardrobe pieces. Cheetah has definitely progressed from being a statement piece to more of a neutral in my eyes. So, for all of my cheetah sisters out there, I’ve rounded up all of my favorite cheetah print autumn wardrobe staples, all under $150!
