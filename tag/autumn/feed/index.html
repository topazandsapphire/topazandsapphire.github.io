]]> Now that #hurricanejoaquin has stopped tormenting the east coast the air has cooled a bit, the pumpkin spices are flowing, and the leaves on the trees can get back to changing. 

In honor of my most favorite season (and since when did loving fall become “basic”?… Let’s all just let that one go so we can get back to breathing in the crisp autumn air sans judgement) I have made a Fall Bucket List. So I present you a list of things I hope to cross off before the radio stations start playing “Grandma Got Run Over by a Reindeer”…

  • Re-read Harry Potter books 1-3.
  • Attend the State Fair.
  • Make homemade pumpkin donuts.
  • Do a corn maze.
  • Go on a canoe or kayak expedition at a local park.
  • Hike somewhere with fall foliage.
  • Go stargazing one night and bring hot chocolate.
  • Go to a museum exhibit.
  • Volunteer at a soup kitchen or homeless shelter.
  • Attend the local symphony.

Is there anything else I should add to my list or any fun fall plans you all have this season? Comment below! Also be sure to follow @topazandsapphire on Instagram to follow our #T&SFallBucketList journey this season.
